personal website

My first personal website in high school during the CE Next Gen camp. The site was created to reflect my personality, with a playful and interactive component. One of its primary features is a pop-up feature: when you hit a button, the character depicting me inside a rocket changes with each click. After achieving a 'lucky number'—roughly 60 clicks—the rocket explodes, causing a change. Curious to see what happens? Feel free to browse and find out!

Face Recognition - Based Smart Lock Door System

As part of a STEM project, a deep learning model trained on a custom dataset for facial recognition was used to develop a smart lock system utilizing Python, OpenCV, and AI. Arduino and ESP32 were used to connect circuits, and a webcam was incorporated from a computer to enable facial recognition. The system ensures safe and effective access control.

Python/OpenCV/Arduino
